Numbers 1-9

3+3+5+4+4+3+5+5+4 = 36

Numbers 10-19

3+6+6+8+8+7+7+9+8+8=70

Numbers 20-99

10*(6+6+5+5+5+7+6+6)+8*36=748

Numbers 1-99

36+70+748=854

Numbers 100-99

1-9 happen 100 times each == 36*100=3600
1-99 happen 9 times == 9*854= 7686
"hundred" happens 9 times == 7*9=63
"hundred and" happens 999 times == 999*10 = 8910

3600+7686+63+8910=20259
